Shape Self Portrait

This is a graphic design piece I made in adobe photoshop during my freshman year of high school. For this project we had to chose a shape to create the entire piece. I chose to make mine out of diamond shapes. For my process I chose a photo of myself and dragged it into photoshop. I then began adding in my diamond shapes and eye dropping the colors from my reference photo.
