Lightning Bulb

This is a project I made in my Intro to Graphic Design class during my second semester of freshman year at UMass Dartmouth. The aim of this project was to create a logo and brand identity for a made up company using some type of insect. I decided to use lightning bugs and create a light bulb with them for my imaginary brand. The first image is of the logo design for the company and the second is the package design for the light bulbs. All the designs were made in Adobe Illustrator.
